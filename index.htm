About Hotel



Royal Bengal Hotel is situated in Chakra Tirtha road beside the Sea beach. It is 3km form Jagannath temple, 5km from the Puri Railway Station and 65km from Biju Patnaik International Airport at Bhubaneswar. We have both AC and Non-AC rooms, from single bed room to four-bed room. We have premium ranges of rooms and suits, which you can avail at a comfortable price. This year, we are celebrating our golden jubilee, 50 years of Royal Bengal Hotel. There are some exciting offers that you can avail, heavy discounts on on-line reservations and bookings. The rooms are well decorated and have attached bathroom. We have a nice dining area, a swimming pool and a bar for some refreshments. 100 to 120 rooms have sea-facing balconies from where you can have the best view of sunrise and sunset. We also have the facility of booking transports for local and outside visit. We always try to give you the best experience from our side.

Tour


Our hotel provides 24 hours car service for local and outside tours. The rates are also reasonable. All the drivers are cooperative and keeps the tourists informed about the tourists sites.

Chilka

Chilika Lake is a brackish water lagoon, spread over the Puri, Khurda and Ganjam districts of Odisha state on the east coast of India.

Puri Jagannath Temple

The Jagannath Temple is an important Hindu temple dedicated to Jagannath, a form of Vishnu, in Puri in the state of Odisha.

Puri Beach

Puri Beach or the Golden beach is a beach in the city of Puri in the state of Odisha, India.

Bhubaneshwar Temple

Bhubaneshwar Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Shiva located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha.

Nandankanan Zoological Park

Nandankanan Zoological Park is a premier large zoo of India. Nandankanan is built right inside the forest and set in a completely natural environment.

Konark Sun Temple

Konark Sun Temple (Konark Surya Mandir) is a 13th-century CE Sun temple at Konark about 35 kilometres northeast from Puri on the coastline of Odisha.
